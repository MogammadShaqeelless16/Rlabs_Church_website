Redemption Church Update (COVID-19)

In this trying time we believe the local church is crucial in bringing “Light to a Dark World”. It’s time for THE CHURCH to “ARISE & SHINE” - Isaiah 60:1. Although our church is not meeting in our buildings physically for now, we know there is no distance in the spirit. We as a church family, are responding to the covid-19 crisis, not by panicking, but by partnering with people, churches and  organizations to show people the love and care of Jesus practically too. Our social justice arm JESUS CARES is involved to help many individuals that are in desperate need and will be active in looking at how to best assist in this time as needs develop.

Sharing the Gospel through technology

You can watch Redemption Church Online via Sunday services, Light in the darkness daily devotion, Redemption Kids content and Redemption Worship.

Over 28 countries are connecting with our content in 4 languages (English, Dutch, Hungarian, Italian and Portuguese will be added shortly). We are seeing daily salvations and testimonies and will be sharing them in a special testimony page soon!

You find our content on www.thisisredemption.com, YouTube and Facebook.

 
 

Jesus Cares Projects

 

South Africa

Already over +4113 Food Buckets, 616 950 Meals , + 3.5 TONS of MAIZE MEAL extra and 85.5 TONS of FOOD IN TOTAL meals were distributed to the families and people in need. We hope to expand this outreach in the coming days.

We have donated supplies to various orphanages with in Gauteng area.

Launching a new partnership with JAM aimed at fighting hunger, poverty and malnutrition in this time. This program will assist to provide food, security and proper nutrition, clean water sources and sanitation as well as emergency relief in the communities that have been hit the hardest during the Covid-19 crisis within South Africa.

For more information about JAM visit website: https://jamint.com ﻿

 

GLOBAL OUTREACH

The Netherlands 

We contributed food stamps for prostitutes living Amsterdam who are without income now due to the red light district being closed down and they are foreign nationals who can’t get home.

We plan to bring gift bags to a mental care hospital and to homeless people.

We connected to a national initiative where our church members can be connected to help requests in their own region through our 6 local churches.

Israel

We are supporting a local ministry Dugit Outreach in Tel Aviv (Ps. Avi Mizrachi)

During Covid-19 they provide food, prayer and the gospel for around 100 families that are in desperate need.

Deliveries to the homes of the elderly and other people of so called ‘risk’ group.

For more information visit Dugit website https://dugit.org

Uganda

We support the ‘Feed My Lamb’ program in Kampala, Uganda (via pastor Moses Mugalasi) and were able to provide food for 30 pastors and their families, who have no income because of lock-down.
